Tattoo Ink Ingredient Decoder

Decode pigment names, CAS numbers, and CI numbers against EU Regulation 2020/2081 (Annex XVII, Entry 75) — the EU restriction on hazardous substances in tattoo inks and permanent make-up.

Accepts pigment trade names, Colour Index (CI) numbers, CAS numbers, and common aliases. Covers banned pigments, restricted additives, and heavy metal limits under EU 2020/2081.

Extracts CI numbers (e.g. CI 74160), CAS numbers (e.g. 147-14-8), and ingredient names. Works with INCI lists, product labels, and SDS Section 3.
